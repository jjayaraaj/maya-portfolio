Data Tables: Grow as they Grow approach

Introduction

This case study focuses on enhancing the user experience of Chargebee's Index Page, with a specific emphasis on accommodating the diverse needs of both startup and enterprise customers. The aim is to streamline the data table interface while expanding its functionality as businesses grow.

All information in this case study is my own and does not necessarily reflect the views of Chargebee. This project was self-initiated and not a part any initiative in Chargebee nor was it published anywhere.

Personas and User needs

There are primarily three main uses for Index Page - Creation, Maintenance and Overview.

Growth Stage Users

  • Expansion of internal teams and focus on function specific job tasks. Example: Finance persona for managing invoices. Support executive handling customer requests.

Conclusion:

Indeed, PLG should be a foundational mindset when approaching problem-solving. A product or feature that's user-friendly and easy to set up leaves a lasting impact, fostering both user growth and retention. In today's tech-driven landscape, a self-serve product offers a substantial market advantage, enabling it to essentially sell itself. My experience with the PLG team has instilled this mindset in me, and I've carried it forward into other projects.
